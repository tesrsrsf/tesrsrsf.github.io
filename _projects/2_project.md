Undergraduate capstone design project on a recipe recommendation system based on user preferences and dietary restrictions. Foodie will recommend recipes based on user's preferences and dietary restrictions, and also provide nutritional information for each recipe. It will also provide the ingredients and instructions for each recipe.

This project was developed with a team.
